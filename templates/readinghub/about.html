Welcome to Reading Hub !



The objective of this application is to organize book events for book lovers and readers based on the books they recommend on the platform.The events will give them the opportunity to meet-up with likeminded fellows, discuss and share their ideas on a particular recommended book after which they rate the book on the application.Users will be able to create account, recommend book by giving a detailed description of the book (type, image etc.), view other suggested books, join and view events, review and rate books. The administrator (event planner) will be able to create events at beautiful scenery based on the list of recommended books on the platform.
